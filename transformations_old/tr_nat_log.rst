==================================
Natural Logarithm Transformation
==================================

-  | .
   | In the formula editor, you can use the ``LN()`` function to calculate natural logarithms for a variable.
   | For example, to calculate natural logarithms for the variable ``score``, enter:

   .. code-block:: text

      LN(score)             
   | You can give the new variable a name, such as ``score_ln``.
-  | The natural logarithm transformation is useful for reducing skewness in data and stabilizing variance.
