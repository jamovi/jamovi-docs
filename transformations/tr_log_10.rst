==============================
Logarithmic 10 Transformations
==============================

-  | .
   | In the formula editor, you can use the ``LOG_10()`` function to calculate the base 10 logarithm for a variable.
   | For example, to calculate the base 10 logarithm for the variable ``score``, enter:

   .. code-block:: text

      LOG_10(score)

   | You can give the new variable a name, such as ``log_10....``.
